FinalSuspect
Switch Appearance

In Final Suspect, we offer players three switchable crewmate appearances, adding more fun and personalized choices：
  1. Classic Bean Mode - The official default, featuring the familiar space bean design you know best.
  2. April Fools' Ranch Mode - A parody style that replaces characters with comical ranch animal designs (inspired by the official April Fools' easter egg).
  3. 愚人节长颈豆模式 - 趣味变体，角色变成脖子超长的长颈豆，致敬官方愚人节特殊外观。
玩家可在设置菜单中自由切换模式。
该功能不影响游戏平衡，仅为娱乐性扩展。
