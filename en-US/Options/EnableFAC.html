FAC

In the Final Suspect mod, we provide an option to enable FAC (Final Anti Cheat).
When this option is enabled,FAC Will start running.

This feature is designed for custom lobbies or mod testing scenarios. Once disabled, the lobby will rely on the honor system among players.

We It is not recommended that you disable FAC.
If you disable the FAC, your lobby may be flooded with cheaters.
