FinalSuspect
Enable Anti-Cheat

In the Final Suspect mod, we provide an option to enable FAC (Final Anti-Cheat).
When this option is enabled, FAC will become active.
This feature is designed for custom lobbies or mod testing scenarios. Once disabled, the lobby will rely on player self-discipline.

We do not recommend that you disable FAC.
禁用FAC后您的房间可能会被外挂炸崩。
