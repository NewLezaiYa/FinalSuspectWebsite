Auto start when lobby is full

In the Final Suspect mod, we have optimized the auto-start logic so that the countdown is triggered when the lobby is nearly full.
When the number of players in the lobby reaches max capacity - 1 (i.e., only one player short of being full), Final Suspect will initiate a 10-second short countdown.
During the countdown, the host can manually cancel.
This mechanism not only avoids delays after the lobby is full, but also prevents the game experience from being affected by the last player joining too late.
