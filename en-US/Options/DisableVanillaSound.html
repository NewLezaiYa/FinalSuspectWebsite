Disable Vanilla Game Music

In Final Suspect, we allow players to mute the lobby waiting music.
When enabled, the relevant scenes will remain silent, suitable for players who wish to play their own music or reduce repetitive audio distractions.
This setting can be adjusted at any time through the mod configuration menu.The feature does not affect game sound effects.

The ambient lobby noise is classified as a sound effect, not music.
