Disable Vanilla Game Music

In Final Suspect, we’ve added a background music control feature that allows players to mute the lobby waiting music.

Once enabled, the related scenes will remain silent, making it suitable for players who prefer using their own music player or want to reduce repetitive audio distractions.
This setting can be adjusted at any time through the mod configuration menu.

This feature does not affect the game’s sound effects.

The lobby waiting noise is categorized as sound effects, rather than music.
