FinalSuspect
Kick Players with Violating Nicknames

When you are the host, if Final Suspect detects that a player is using a violating nickname (e.g., one containing sensitive words or other names that ruin the gaming experience), the system will automatically kick that player. This feature effectively maintains lobby order by preventing disruptive names from causing interference to other players. You can manually disable the auto-kick feature in the settings.
