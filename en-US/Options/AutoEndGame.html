Auto return to lobby when game ends

When a game round ends, the Final Suspect Auto return to lobby when game ends feature will automatically trigger, skipping the settlement animation and post-game replay page, and directly redirect players back to the game lobby interface.
By reducing unnecessary transition steps, this design effectively improves operational smoothness during consecutive matches. It is especially useful in scenarios that require frequent rounds, such as Hide and Seek or Practice Mode.
