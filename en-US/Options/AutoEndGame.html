FinalSuspect
结束时自动返回大厅

When a match ends, Final Suspect’s Auto return to lobby when game ends feature will be triggered. It automatically skips the result animation and summary page, taking players directly back to the game lobby.
By reducing unnecessary transition steps, this design effectively improves operational smoothness during consecutive matches. It is especially useful in scenarios that require frequent rounds, such as Hide and Seek or Practice Mode.
