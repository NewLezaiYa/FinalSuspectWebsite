Display player platform and client information

When this option is enabled, the platform affiliation (e.g., Steam, Epic) and friend code of all players will be displayed beneath their names while in the lobby.

This not only allows players to easily see the platform source of room members but also helps the development team quickly identify potential cheaters—when players report hacking, the friend code can be submitted as key data, making it easier for the devs to track and handle violating accounts.

During live streaming or content recording, it is recommended to disable this feature to protect your privacy.
