Bean Skin Selector

In Final Suspect, we provide players with three switchable Bean avatars, adding more fun and personalization options：

  1. Classic Bean Mode – The official default, featuring the familiar astronaut Bean look.
  2. April Fools Steed Mode – A gag style that replaces characters with comical Steed avatars (based on the official April Fools’ Easter egg).
  3. April Fools Long-neck Bean Mode – A fun variant where characters turn into Long-neck Beans, paying homage to the official April Fools’ special appearance.

Players can freely switch modes in the settings menu.
This feature does not affect game balance and is purely an entertainment-oriented addition.
