FinalSuspect Update

自动更新

Final Suspect has a built-in update checker within the game, and will automatically check for updates each time you launch it.
When an update is available, you will be required to update; otherwise, you will not be able to continue playing.

The update check logic differs between the Release and Open Beta versions ：
The Release version will not require you to update to the Open Beta version，Open Beta version does not perform update checks.

手动更新

如非必要，我们建议您优先选择自动更新。

若遇上更新检查失败/下载出现问题，可按以下步骤手动更新：

  1. 访问GitHub Releases，下载最新版本的dll文件。
  2. 打开Among Us根目录，再继续打开BepInEx\plugins文件夹。
  3. 将下载的dll文件替换掉原有的dll文件。
  4. 至此，更新结束。
