自动导出

您可以在Final Suspect功能中选择输出日志，随后Final Suspect将自动打开输出日志所在的文件夹。

For an introduction to the automatic log export function, please refer to this document.

The output directory of Final Suspect is located in the following path：

%APPDATA%
\..\LocalLow\Innersloth\Among Us\FinalSuspect\Logs\Final Suspect-logs

You can pressWinR, and paste this path to open it manually.

手动查看

If you can't open the settings page, you can also choose to view the log manually.

Launch Steam, go to your Library, select Among Us, click the settings icon on the right, choose Manage, and then select Browse Local Files.Steam will open to the Among Us root directory.

Open the BepInEx folder, and the LogOutput.log file is the log file.该文件将随着你重新启动Final Suspect自动清除。
